What Language?

Hi, I am a simple language detector!

I was created by:

I have been taught to distinguish between the following languages:
English,  French,  German,  Romanian

I trained by feeding a TensorFlow Nerual Network quite a few Wikipedia articles (about 15 per language) written in the languages listed above. I try to guess the language of the word you ask me by checking how it scores against the algorithms I learned from doing this.

I'm not a very good guesser, I think maybe if I trained harder I could do better!
